# Gate Level Minimization

## Boolean Functions and Representation 
1. Sum of Product form (SOP) 
	- aka Disjunctive Normal Form 
	- $$ f(A,B,C) = (A\bar B + \bar B C) $$
1. Product of sums form (POS) 
	- aka Conjunctive Normal form 
	-  $$ f(A,B,C) = (\bar A + \bar B)(B + C) $$
